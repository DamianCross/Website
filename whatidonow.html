What I Do Now

I work for Medtronic in Woburn, Massachusetts where our Minimally Invasive Therapies Group (MITG) has its gynecology division. We are helping gynecologists adapt to the shift of bringing surgical procedures out of the operating room and into the physician’s office.


Our current product is the TruClear™ system, which is used in See & Treat Hysteroscopy. Our system gives surgeons the capability to remove a large range of intrauterine abnormailities. TruClear™ includes a hysteroscopic tissue removal system, hysteroscopes, tissue resection devices, and the HysteroLux™ Fluid Management System. Learn more here.
